Business Model

The game has been targeted towards students who are usually under stress in their respective fields. The buyers could be the students themselves or even the management of the educational institute. The final product will include the mount for the hanging along with two forms. The mechanism of the game will be set up. There will be various versions of the movement that will keep getting updated regularly in case the organisation subscribes for it.

What the product will include:
  • Suspended objects with movements
  • Paper planes to throw
  • The pulley mechanisms
  • The stepper motor and ultrasonic sensors connections made
  • Posters to understand the game well before playing
Target audience:
  • Students under stress
  • Students that need a break
  • Working professionals
Where we plan to sell it:
  • To educational institutes
  • Coaching classes like ccpt, pace, etc.
Possible buyers:
  • Educational institutes
  • Students
  • Parents of the students under huge stress
How we plan to sell it:
  • Advertisings to attract the audience
  • Few retail locations
  • Online stores
  • Have an option for renting it out for a fixed period of time
  • Free trials before buying
Pricing:
  • The pricing stratergy would be to start the price high and as the product gets older drop the price.
  • This strategy could be extended all the way to eventually re-realising a game as a part of a bundle of games as well.
  • Rental pricing would be based on the time period that the game will be borrowed for I.e. more the time more the cost.
Distribution channels:
  • Retail outlets
  • Ecommerce websites
  • Malls with stores like hamleys, crosswords and others
  • Launch business through crowdfunding sites such as Kickstarter. Since, it is the world's largest funding platform for creative projects ,launching a business on a crowdfunding site would not only let business collect funds that can be used to pay for manufacturing expenses and create awareness. The publicity of a crowdfunding campaign can create momentum that will generate more sales.
Promotion:
  • In long term, get involved with and advertise to specialised social media groups, or at bars that host tournaments. 
  • Website or print ads that highlight the fun and friendly competition.
  • Entering into some competition can help boost sales if a seal is displayed on the game’s packaging.
  • Taking business to local corporations as a way for recreation among busy professionals.
Legal Consideration:
  • State and Local Business Licenses for the operation of the company. Collection of sales tax on the services provided has to be kept in mind.
  • Trademark and Copyright Protection- For protection of rights of the uniqueness of the concept or the product.
  • Certificate of Occupancy- For operation of the business in a particular location
  • Services Contract- Contract with the educational institutions.
